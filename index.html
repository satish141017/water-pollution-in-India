Water is the primary and essential need of every living organism. It is the most precious gift by nature that helps in survival. 70% of our body is filled with water..Water is a fundamental element that is essential for human survival. It plays a critical role in maintaining the proper functioning of various bodily processes. Water aids in digestion, absorption, and transportation of nutrients and oxygen throughout the body. It helps regulate body temperature and lubricates joints, reducing the risk of injuries. Water also acts as a natural detoxifier by flushing out waste products and toxins from the body. It promotes healthy skin, preventing dryness and wrinkles. Proper hydration is essential for optimal health, and it is recommended to drink enough water daily.
